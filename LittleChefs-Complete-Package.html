🎮 How to Use the App

1 Choose a Character: Pick from 12 beautiful characters (5 girls, 5 boys, 2 magical)
2 Enter Your Name: Kids can personalize their experience
3 Select Age: Choose between 5-12 years for age-appropriate content
4 Search Any Food: Type anything like "beef nihari", "pizza", "cookies", etc.
5 Follow Steps: Interactive step-by-step instructions with mini-games!

🔒 Safety Features

  • ✅ All recipes emphasize asking grown-ups for help with heat and sharp objects
  • ✅ Age-appropriate language for kids 5-12
  • ✅ No external API calls - works completely offline
  • ✅ No data collection or tracking
  • ✅ Kid-friendly sound effects (no loud/scary sounds)

💡 Tips for Parents & Teachers

  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Supervise cooking: Always be present when kids use the stove or oven
  • 📚 Learning opportunity: Teach measuring, fractions, and following instructions
  • 🌍 Cultural exploration: Try recipes from different cuisines together
  • 🎯 Build confidence: Start with "Super Easy" recipes and work up
  • 📸 Document the fun: Take photos of their creations!

🛠️ Troubleshooting

App won't open?

Make sure you're using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge). The standalone HTML file works offline!

Sounds not working?

Some browsers require user interaction before playing audio. Click anywhere on the page first.

Text-to-speech not working?

Make sure your device has speech synthesis enabled. Most modern browsers support this.
